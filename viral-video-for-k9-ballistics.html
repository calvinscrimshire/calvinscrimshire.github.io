Viral Video For K9 Ballistics

I tried to make a viral dog video and it worked.

My goal was to increase engagement on Facebook, get people to share the video, and get free views by going viral. It was a long shot, but possible I thought.

The comments section is absolute gold. People tag each-other, don't get the joke, then make fun of each-other not getting the joke. It's a creative piece of advertising that lets us have fun with our audience!

The performance was absolutely amazing as well. We earned $.06 per landing page view. That's nothing! Results like that are very difficult to achieve and I'm super proud to share them here.
