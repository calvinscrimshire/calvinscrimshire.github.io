One Fast Cat Kickstarter Launch

In 2013 I embarked down the wild rabbit hole that is Kickstarter.

My boss had just completed a 3 year development cycle of the One Fast Cat Wheel. It's a revolutionary engineering upgrade to existing cat exercise wheels that makes it ship much smaller and lighter, bringing the cost down and giving access to millions of people who couldn't afford the previous generation of cat wheels.

The tooling for plastic injection molding is quite expensive so we decided to offset the costs by doing a Kickstarter launch first, instead of building an Ecommerce website only.

Our stated goal was to raise $10,000 and we exceeded that by quite a bit. In the end we raised over $340,000 in 60 days and blew away our expectations.

The product went viral and many customers outside of our core audience target became interested in the wheel. Now it's a thriving international brand!

I learned so much from this successful project:

  • Bring your own audience
  • Aim high and create stretch goals
  • Keep up with public facing information like FAQs, it could save your brand
  • Be open to discovering new opportunities that don't match the initial hypothesis
